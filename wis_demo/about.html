About

Learn about the Construct Your Career initiative and how it helps people explore the construction industry.

What is Construct Your Career?

Construct Your Career is an interactive platform designed to introduce people to the diverse range of careers available in the construction industry. Through immersive mini games, career exploration tools, and engaging content, we aim to inspire the next generation of builders.

Our Mission

We believe that construction offers rewarding, well-paid careers that are often overlooked. Our mission is to break down barriers, challenge stereotypes, and show that there's a place for everyone in the construction industry.

What You Can Do Here

  • Explore different construction career pathways
  • Play mini games that simulate real construction tasks
  • Learn about career progression from entry-level to senior roles
  • Discover what skills and qualifications you need

Who Is This For?

Whether you're a student considering your options, a career changer looking for something new, or an educator helping others find their path — this platform is built for you.
