Dispensing Opticians

 

Here at Howick Village Optometrists our highly trained team is on hand 6 days a week providing comprehensive eye examinations using advanced clinical technologies and decades of joint professional experience. Experience that enables us to listen, accurately interpret what you say thereby ensuring you always receive the right advice and prescription which results in the best possible vision for you - in short 'we listen so you can see'.

Optimum eye care has always been a priority for us. That’s why we employ highly qualified optometrists to provide extensive eye examinations. We pride ourselves on having the latest ZEISS optical equipment, including retinal camera, OCT and Visual Fields which enables us to screen and monitor diseases such as diabetes retinopathy, glaucoma, and macular degeneration etc. Our optometrists will assess the quality of your vision as well as thoroughly examine the health of your eyes.

The first step to better eye health and clearer vision is booking in an appointment here.
