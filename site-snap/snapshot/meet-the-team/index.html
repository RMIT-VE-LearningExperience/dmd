Dispensing Optician
An optician is a technician who uses the prescription provided by an optometrist or ophthalmologist to deliver suitable spectacle lenses and frames (if needed) best suited for the users requirements.

Optometrist
Is a health service provider, involved exclusively with vision problems. They have completed a 5 year Optometry degree at Auckland University of Optometry. They determine visual acuity and will provide a prescription (if required) for spectacle lenses or contact lenses. A comprehensive eye health check is also provided.

Receptionist / Optical Assistant
Not only your meet and greet but also able to provide assistance with suitable frame selection.
