About Us

Howick Village Optometrists is proud to be the oldest business in Howick Village. Frith and Graham Jenkins are now proud owners of this local independent optometry business. Originally set up in the early 1960s by Graham’s father, Bryan, in the family home at the top of Picton Street. Bryan was one of the youngest qualified optometrists in England and brought his skills and his passion for optometry to New Zealand.

Graham joined his father as a Dispensing Optician and has been working in the practice for 50 years. Graham has seen many changes in the optical industry from fashion in eyewear to the development of spectacle lenses. Because of his extensive knowledge Graham is able to work with you to achieve optimum results to suit your visual requirements and is able to help you find the perfect fit in frames.

The practice moved into the heart of Howick, to the centre of Picton Street, with two beautiful windows to display the latest in eyewear fashion and an impressive floor space to host the largest selection of frames in the country. Now the practice has grown to have three testing rooms, two dispensing areas and the latest optical equipment including Zeiss Retinal Camera and Zeiss Cirrus OCT 6000.

Frith joined HVO in 2003 after spending the previous 20 years in sales. Frith is now the practice manager and is enjoying being involved in the business and the community of Howick. Frith has become known for her honesty when working with clients when selecting a frame and loves a challenge! You’ll also now find their daughter Purdie working here at HVO, bringing in the third generation to the business. 

At Howick Village Optometrists our highly trained team of professionals is on hand six days a week providing comprehensive eye exams using advanced clinical technologies and decades of professional experience. Experience that enables us to listen, accurately interpret what you say thereby ensuring you always receive the right advice and prescription which results in the best possible vision for you - in short “we listen so you can see”.
